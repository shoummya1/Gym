Help

FAQ

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes. Plus and Elite members can freeze once per year for up to 3 months for travel or medical reasons — talk to the front desk.

Is there a joining fee?

We run seasonal promotions. Use the contact form for current signup offers and corporate rates.

Do you offer student or corporate discounts?

We partner with local schools and employers. Email hello@ironandmotion.example with proof of eligibility.

What should I bring on day one?

Comfortable clothes, indoor shoes, a water bottle, and a towel if you’re on Essential. Lockers accept your own padlock or our smart bands.
